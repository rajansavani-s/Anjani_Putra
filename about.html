Our Story

Building The Future Through 3D Printing

Anjani Putra was established with a simple vision.

To make advanced manufacturing and custom product creation accessible to everyone.

From engineering prototypes and mechanical components to educational models and personalized gifts, we help customers transform digital concepts into real world products.

Our focus is on precision, quality, innovation and customer satisfaction. Every project receives attention to detail from concept development to final production.

Whether you are a student, startup, engineer, manufacturer or gift retailer, we provide reliable 3D printing solutions tailored to your needs.
